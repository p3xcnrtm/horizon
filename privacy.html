Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Horizon Capital collects, uses, shares, and protects your information. It is designed to reflect common practices among leading global investment managers.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide services, meet legal obligations, manage risk, and improve your experience. This includes:

  • Identification and contact data (e.g., name, address, email, phone, government ID where required by law)
  • Financial and suitability data (e.g., investment objectives, risk tolerance, income, net worth, source of funds)
  • Transactional and account data (e.g., holdings, orders, statements, funding instructions)
  • Device and usage data (e.g., IP address, browser type, pages visited, cookies)
  • Communications and support interactions

2. How We Use Information

  • Deliver, administer, and improve our services
  • Perform due diligence, KYC/AML, sanctions screening, and risk monitoring
  • Comply with laws, regulations, and audit requirements
  • Communicate about accounts, markets, products, and updates
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, security incidents, and abuse
  • Perform analytics, research, and product development

3. Legal Bases

Depending on your jurisdiction, our processing relies on one or more of: performance of a contract, legitimate interests, compliance with legal obligations, and, where applicable, consent.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share information with:

  • Affiliates and service providers (e.g., custodians, brokers, administrators, IT vendors, analytics)
  • Professional advisors (e.g., auditors, legal counsel)
  • Regulators, law enforcement, and courts, as required
  • In connection with corporate transactions (e.g., mergers, acquisitions)

We do not sell personal information. We do not share for targeted advertising without appropriate notices and choices as required by law.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enable site functionality, measure performance, and personalize content. You can control cookies via your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards aligned with industry standards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide services, meet legal and regulatory obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

8. International Transfers

If information is transferred across borders, we implement appropriate safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses) consistent with applicable law.

9. Your Rights

Subject to local laws, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your information, object to processing, withdraw consent, or request portability. To exercise rights, contact us as set out below.

10. Children

Our services are not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
